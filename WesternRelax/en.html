About us

At the boundary of the Notecka Forest, 15 km from Wronki, in direct vicinity of three lakes, there is a picturesque village of KAROLEWO with a charming spacious agritourist farm, perfect for those wanting to enjoy full relaxation.
Have a holiday in an interesting and beautiful area of the Wielkopolska region. It is a paradise for enthusiasts of picking mushrooms, anglers, tourists, and people looking for peace and quiet.
